# Trainer Analytics Recommendations

## ðŸ“Š Financial Analytics (Priority 1)

### Revenue Metrics
- **Total Revenue**: All-time revenue from all clients
- **Monthly Revenue**: Current month and previous months
- **Revenue Trends**: Line chart showing revenue over time (daily/weekly/monthly)
- **Average Revenue Per Client (ARPC)**: Total revenue / number of clients
- **Revenue by Payment Type**: Breakdown of one-time vs subscription payments
- **Revenue Forecast**: Projected revenue based on current subscriptions

### Payment Analytics
- **Payment Success Rate**: % of successful payments vs failed
- **Payment Methods**: Breakdown by payment type (card, bank transfer, etc.)
- **Outstanding Payments**: Clients with pending or overdue payments
- **Payment Trends**: Average payment amount, frequency
- **Refund Rate**: % of payments refunded

### Subscription Analytics
- **Active Subscriptions**: Current active subscriptions count
- **Subscription Revenue**: Monthly recurring revenue (MRR)
- **Churn Rate**: % of clients who cancelled subscriptions
- **Average Subscription Duration**: How long clients stay subscribed
- **Subscription Tiers**: Revenue breakdown by subscription tier/package
- **Upgrade/Downgrade Trends**: Clients moving between tiers

### Client Financial Metrics
- **Top Paying Clients**: Clients generating most revenue
- **Client Lifetime Value (CLV)**: Total revenue per client over time
- **Payment History per Client**: Individual client payment timeline

## ðŸ“Š Visualization Recommendations

### Charts & Graphs
- **Line Charts**: Trends over time (revenue, clients, engagement)
- **Bar Charts**: Comparisons (monthly revenue, client counts)
- **Pie Charts**: Breakdowns (payment types, subscription tiers)
- **Area Charts**: Cumulative metrics (total revenue, client growth)
- **Heatmaps**: Activity patterns (workout days, check-in times)
- **Gauges**: Completion rates, success metrics

### Dashboard Layout
- **Top Section**: Key metrics (KPI cards)
- **Middle Section**: Trend charts (revenue, clients, engagement)
- **Bottom Section**: Detailed breakdowns and tables
- **Filters**: Date range, client selection, metric selection
